## Creative Assets Specifications

### Image Posts
- **Dimensions**: 1200 x 628 px (LinkedIn optimal)
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Branding**: Logo + tagline ("Automating the Automation")
- **Colors**: Primary green (#16a34a), blue (#2563eb), gray (#1f2937)
- **Typography**: Bold sans-serif headlines, clean body text

### Video Posts
- **Duration**: 30-90 seconds (optimal engagement)
- **Format**: MP4, H.264 codec
- **Resolution**: 1920 x 1080 (Full HD)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 for feed, 1:1 for mobile
- **Captions**: Always included (85% watch without sound)
- **Branding**:
  - Opening slate: Logo + tagline (3 seconds)
  - Watermark: Bottom right corner
  - Closing CTA: Website + call-to-action (5 seconds)

### Carousel Posts
- **Slides**: 5-10 slides optimal
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1080 px per slide
- **Flow**: Problem → Solution → Benefits → Proof → CTA
